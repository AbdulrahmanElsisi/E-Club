Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-Club?

E-Club is a student-led club at Nile University that promotes entrepreneurship by hosting events, workshops, and activities to help students develop their ideas and turn them into real projects.

Do I have to be a Nile University student to join?

No, most of our events are open to everyone, but some activities are exclusively for Nile University students.

Do I need to have a startup idea before joining?

Not at all! You can join to learn, get inspired, and develop your idea during our events and training programs.

How can I join the club?

Membership opens through an application form we share on our official platforms, or you can sign up at our booths on campus during recruitment periods.

Are the events free?

Most events are free, but some specialized workshops or programs may have a small fee to cover costs.

What are the main events organized by the club?

We host hackathons, the E-Gnite competition, workshops, networking sessions with entrepreneurs, and visits to startups.

Does the club provide financial support for projects?

We offer training, mentorship, and networking opportunities with investors and companies, but direct funding depends on available programs and partnerships.

How can I stay updated about the club’s activities?

Follow us on our social media accounts or check our official website for the latest news and events.

Do you provide participation or attendance certificates?

Yes, for some events and workshops we provide certificates accredited by the club or our partners.

Can I volunteer to help organize events even if I’m not a member?

Sometimes, we open volunteer opportunities for non-members depending on the type of event and our needs.
