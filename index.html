Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-Gnite?

E-Gnite is a 3-day entrepreneurial event that brings together students, professionals, and experts to learn, network, and compete in innovation challenges. It's designed to ignite your entrepreneurial journey through workshops, mentorship, and hands-on experience.

Who can participate?

E-Gnite is open to university students, recent graduates, and young professionals interested in entrepreneurship. No prior experience is required - we welcome participants from all backgrounds and skill levels.

How much does it cost to attend?

E-Gnite is completely free to attend! All workshops, materials, and meals during the event are provided at no cost to participants. We believe in making entrepreneurship education accessible to everyone.

What should I bring?

Please bring your laptop, charger, and enthusiasm! We'll provide all workshop materials and resources. Comfortable clothing is recommended as you'll be actively participating in sessions.

How do I register?

Registration will open soon! Click the "Register Now" button on our website to sign up. Spaces are limited, so we encourage early registration to secure your spot.

Will there be prizes?

Yes! The winning teams will receive cash prizes, mentorship opportunities, and potential investment connections. Additionally, all participants will receive certificates and valuable networking opportunities.
